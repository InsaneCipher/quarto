A browser-based implementation of the classic abstract strategy game Quarto, playable by two players sharing a single mouse.

**[Click to Play](https://insanecipher.github.io/quarto/)**


## How to Play

Quarto is a two-player game played on a 4×4 board with 16 unique pieces. Each piece has four binary attributes: **dark/light**, **round/square**, **big/small**, and **hollow/solid**.

**Turns alternate in two phases:**

1. **Give** — choose a piece from the tray to hand to your opponent
2. **Place** — place the piece your opponent gave you onto the board, then pick the next piece for them

**Win** by placing the fourth piece in any row, column, or diagonal where all four pieces share at least one attribute. Call **QUARTO!**
